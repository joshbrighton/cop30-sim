COP30 Media Training Simulation

This interactive simulation is meant to prepare Government of Canada spokespeople for media interviews on COP30. To navigate to the simulation, click the button below. You may be prompted to turn your microphone on.

To start the simulation, simply say, “Ok, I’m ready,” and the interview will begin. Once the simulation is complete, the bot will provide detailed feedback on your performance. You do not need to acknowledge the bot — just close the simulation tab.
